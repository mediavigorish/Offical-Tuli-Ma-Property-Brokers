Strategic Commercial Spaces in Zambia

Under the leadership of Kaseya Chabuka, Tuli Ma Property Brokers facilitates the sales, acquisition, and leasing of commercial structures with high compliance standards.

We help companies locate highly functional headquarters, high-capacity warehouses, retail fronts with heavy foot traffic, hotel layouts, and commercial-zoned construction land. Every asset is carefully researched for legal compliance, accessibility, and utility strength.

High-Capacity Warehouses

Industrial warehouses, logistics distribution hubs, and cold-storage units in key logistics nodes around Kafue Road and Airport corridors.

Executive Office Buildings

From single-floor premium corporate offices in Longacres to entire self-contained headquarters structures equipped with boardrooms and security gates.

Premium Retail Property

Highly visible showrooms, shopping strip spaces, boutique sites, and restaurant locations matching heavy vehicle and pedestrian flow parameters.

Hotels & Hospitality Blocks

Brokerage for established lodges, luxury boutique hotels, and guesthouse layouts ripe for takeover, expansions, or asset re-purposing.

Zoned Commercial Land
