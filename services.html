What We Do

Professional Brokerage Services

Experienced Property Brokers with a Passion for Helping Clients Sell Or Find Their Dream Homes

We offer standard-setting real estate brokerage solutions in Lusaka. For property owners, we market your listings with premium strategies. For seekers, our search consultancy is completely free.

Property Listings

We list your houses, plots, offices, and warehouses. Our properties are syndicated across premium directories and networks for maximum visibility.

Residential Sales

Helping families purchase secure townhouses, family homes, and plots. We verify all title documents to ensure safe, legally compliant transactions.

Commercial Property

Brokerage for premium office locations, retail spaces, high-density complexes, and hotels in key business districts of Lusaka.

Luxury Homes

We curate outstanding high-end villas and custom mansions equipped with swimming pools, solar arrays, and robust security installations.

Fully Furnished Apartments

We manage and rent executive, short-term and long-term furnished apartments with premium maid, security, and utility integrations.

Farm Sales

Need agricultural land? We broker highly fertile farm plots, small holdings, and expansive acreage inside and around Kafue and Lusaka Province.

Warehouse Sales

Heavy-duty industrial warehouses, logistics depots, cold-storage units, and distribution complexes under flexible terms.

Commercial Plots

Prime roadside plots with zoning permits for immediate commercial construction, showrooms, shopping strip development, or storage.

Investment Properties

We analyze and broker residential apartment blocks and retail units with high cap-rates and reliable, long-term yield projections.

Client Property Search

Tell us your specifications and budget; we scan the entire market, organize private viewings, and source matching locations 100% free.

Property Marketing

Deploying high-definition video walkthroughs, premium photography, social media marketing, and targeted search engine campaigns to find buyers fast.

Deal Negotiation

We coordinate direct, transparent discussions between buyers and sellers to establish fair price structures, flexible schedules, and sound deposits.

Transaction Guidance

Comprehensive oversight through legal checks, title deed verification, government taxes, local authority registration, and contract sign-offs.

Property Consulting

Strategic advisory on buy-vs-rent decisions, residential location assessments, commercial rental projections, and construction site selection.

Rental Assistance

Ensuring lease agreements protect tenant and landlord rights. We coordinate key handovers, walk-through inspections, and security deposit management.
