Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Tuli Ma Property Brokers, we collect information you provide directly to us through enquiry forms, viewing requests, and listing submission processes. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any details concerning real estate holdings you want marketed or purchased.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to facilitate the search and transaction processes under your specific authorization. This includes coordinating viewing tours, syndicating your approved property listings, communicating deals via WhatsApp or phone, and drafting leasing or sales agreements.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and never sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share relevant details with direct counter-parties (e.g., sharing a buyer's phone number with a seller during direct negotiations) with your explicit consent.

4. Security of Data

We employ robust administrative and digital security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. All files, photos, and title deed scans submitted via the request listing module are held securely.

5. Your Consent & Choices

By utilizing our website, listings system, and enquiry modules, you consent to our collection, processing, and storage practices as outlined in this document. If you wish to request the deletion or modification of your information, reach out to kaseyamc@gmail.com directly.
