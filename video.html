Commercial vs. Residential Real Estate: Unveiling the Distinctions

Commercial real estate encompasses properties and spaces primarily used for business activities, such as offices, retail stores, industrial complexes, and hotels. Unlike residential real estate, which is designed for personal living, commercial properties are intended for generating income through rent or capital appreciation. Understanding the contrast between the two is key to successful investing and property management.
